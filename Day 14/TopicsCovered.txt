Time Series Forecasting: 

Time series forecasting is a technique that uses historical data to predict future values. It is a type of predictive modeling that is used to forecast future values of
    1. Moving Average Rolling - no Exponential
       -   Trailing Rolling Mean 
    2. Single Exponential Smoothing (Single)
    4. Holt's Linear Method (Double)
    5. Holt's Exponential Method (Double)
    6. Damped Add and Multi (Double)
    7. Additive Seasonal HW (Triple)
    8. Multi Seasonal HW (Triple)
